Bio

Dong Zhen completed his PhD in computer science from Heidelberg University in 2017, advised by Prof. Artur Andrzejak . After his PhD he joined in the team of Prof. Abhik Roychoudhury at National University of Singapore as a postdoctoral researcher. His research is focused on testing, vulnerability detection, and repair of software systems.
